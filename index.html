Summary

Salutations from a website that covers an essential topic of trigonometry, Trigonometric Identities! We chose to cover this topic because we think that it plays a very important role in the Trigonometry world! Since it leads to other huge topics, such as graphing and the Laws of Sine and Cosine, we thought it would be best to start from the beginning, making it the most important topic!


There are two things you can do with trigonometry identity problems, you can either solve or verify the expressions.


To solve trigonometry problems, you are isolating the variable to one side and on the other side of the equal sign will be your answer. Identities can be used to simplify or cancel out expressions as the problem or answer will be longer.


To verify trigonometry problems, you are proving that both expressions equal the same to each other by simplifying one side so that it would equal to the same expression on the other side of the equation. Identities make this process easier!
